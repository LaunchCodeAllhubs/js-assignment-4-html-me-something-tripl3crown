"Overview:"



"I am eagerly anticipating the culinary delights, captivating attractions, and shopping experiences that my friends and I will savor during our visit to Tokyo, Kyoto, Nara, and Osaka this fall. Each place holds a special allure for us."

Tokyo

"Tokyo is renowned for its vibrant nightlife, dynamic atmosphere, cutting-edge technology, and captivating attractions, including the TeamLab Museum, Akihabara and Ginza shopping districts, pub crawls, Imperial Palace, and many more exciting destinations."

Osaka

"In Osaka, we eagerly anticipate exploring its lively nightlife, relishing the delectable street food offered by vendors with vibrant personalities, and staying at unique hotels."

Kyoto

"In Kyoto, we look forward to immersing ourselves in traditional Japanese culture and customs, including the enchanting experience of staying at a ryokan (traditional inn) and indulging in the natural hot springs found in numerous onsens."

Nara

"Nara presents us with a delightful and extraordinary opportunity to interact with deer in a way that feels magical and impossible in the US. The residents' respectful and kind treatment of the deer allows them to fearlessly approach and be petted by humans. Their friendliness is heartwarming, and they often bow their heads in appreciation after receiving treats from visitors and tourists."
